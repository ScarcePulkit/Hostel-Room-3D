## Here are the keys:
### To move Eye
w: Up  <br />
s: Down <br />
a: Left<br />
d: Right<br />
i: Zoom in<br />
o: Zoom out<br />

### To move Camera Point
u: Up <br />
j: Down<br />
h: Left<br />
k: Right<br />

Press r to move to default position

### For Lighting
Light Source 1: Press 1 to turn on/off<br />
Light Source 2: Press 2 to turn on/off
